More Than a Cup.
It's a Ritual.

Founded in 2019 by two Hong Kong coffee obsessives, Brew & Co. was born from a simple belief: that every cup should be an experience worth savouring. We source exclusively from sustainable, single-origin farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Taiwan, working directly with growers who share our uncompromising standards.

Our baristas are trained to treat each brew as a craft — from the precise grind to the final pour — ensuring that whether you order an espresso or a cold brew, it arrives as a small, perfect moment in your day.

Nestled on Hollywood Road in Central, our café is a warm refuge from the city's pace — a place to slow down, connect, and let great coffee do the talking.
